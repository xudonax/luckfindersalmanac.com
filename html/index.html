Welcome to Luckfinder's Almanac of the Great Forest

Enter the untamed lands of the great forest. You will find yourself considerably smaller in this 5e compatible campaign setting. All the human, elves and other giant races are all but gone from the world. Jump in the life of one of the small critters or fairies, as you walk, jump, climb or fly between the towering trees. Hiding high up in the foliage of the trees from a huge cat prowling the forest floor, hoping it won't stumble upon your hidden home. I am Silvana Suurmond and I'm writing up this world for the most popular role-playing game. Please follow me along my process to be kept updated on how it's going and hopefully to it's final release. For now I'm hard at work breathing life into Shimmering Luckfinder, your guide into this new world. This well travelled deep pixie has all the knowledge to teach you how to survive. I currently run my games at the amazing Dutch TTRPG Discord server Dutch20.
